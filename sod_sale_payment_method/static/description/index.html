SOD Sale Payment Method

Description

This module adds the payment method on the Customer and the Sale Order.

Functionality

After activating this module, users will be able to find the 'Sale Payment Method' field under the Sales & Purchase tab in the contact. This field will list only the Bank and Cash Journals. Users can add a payment method to the customer.

The Payment Method is also available in the Sale Order, and it will be updated based on the Customer selected.
